super ludo is at first a console based game


Rules

the players a dealt a color and the one who starts are choosen randomly

the pieces are always on the board but can have another state

you get 3 tries to get one "ude" to state = move if you don't have any pieces with state = move
if you roll a 6 you get a piece that have state = ude to change to state = move

you may boot another piece "home" if it is your own

you may not pass another of your pieces
if you try to move anothers piece you will loose your turn
